1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

Solitairefun is designed with privacy at its core. We do NOT collect, store, or process any personal information such as:

  • Names, email addresses, or phone numbers
  • Account credentials or login information
  • Payment or financial information
  • Physical addresses or location data
  • Social security numbers or government-issued IDs

Since our games require no registration or sign-up, you can enjoy our services completely anonymously.

1.2 Technical Information

We automatically collect certain technical information when you visit our Site:

  • Browser Information: Browser type, version, and language settings
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and screen resolution
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent on pages, and general navigation patterns
  • IP Address: Your Internet Protocol address for security and analytical purposes

This information is collected anonymously and used solely to improve our services and ensure site functionality.

2. Cookies and Similar Technologies

2.1 What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help websites remember information about your visit. We use minimal, essential cookies to provide basic functionality.

2.2 How We Use Cookies

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly:

  • Session management for game state
  • Security and fraud prevention
  • Site performance optimization
  • Preference settings (e.g., game difficulty)

Local Storage

We use browser local storage to save your game progress and settings locally on your device. This data never leaves your browser and is automatically deleted when you clear your browser data.

2.3 Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling cookies may affect the functionality of our games. Most browsers allow you to:

  • View what cookies are stored and delete them individually or all at once
  • Block third-party cookies
  • Block cookies from specific sites
  • Accept all cookies by default
  • Be notified when a cookie is issued

3. Third-Party Services

3.1 Analytics

We may use third-party analytics services to understand how users interact with our Site. These services may collect information such as how often users visit the Site, what pages they visit, and what other sites they used prior to coming to our Site. We use this information only to improve our Site and services.

The information collected is aggregated and anonymized. Analytics providers may use cookies to track your activities across websites, but we do not have access to any personally identifiable information from these services.

3.2 Content Delivery Networks (CDNs)

We use CDNs to deliver content quickly and efficiently. These services may process your IP address but do not collect or store personal information on our behalf.

3.3 No Data Selling

We do NOT sell, rent, or share any user data with third parties for marketing purposes. We do NOT provide your information to advertisers or data brokers.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect the minimal data we collect:

  • HTTPS Encryption: All data transmitted between your browser and our servers is encrypted using SSL/TLS
  • Regular Security Audits: We regularly review our security practices and update them as needed
  • Access Controls: Only authorized personnel have access to server logs and analytics data
  • Data Minimization: We collect only the minimum data necessary for site functionality

However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect data, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

5. Children's Privacy

Our Site is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected any data from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete that information as quickly as possible.

Parents and guardians should supervise their children's online activities. If you believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately at contact@Solitairefun.site.

We encourage parents to teach their children about safe internet use and to monitor their online activities.

6. Your Privacy Rights

6.1 Access and Control

Because we don't collect personal information, there is no personal data to access, modify, or delete. Your game progress is stored locally in your browser, and you have full control over it:

  • Clear your browser cache to delete game data
  • Use your browser's privacy mode to play without storing any data
  • Disable cookies through browser settings (may affect functionality)

6.2 Do Not Track Signals

We respect Do Not Track (DNT) browser settings. When DNT is enabled, we will not use tracking cookies for analytics purposes.

6.3 California Privacy Rights

California residents have the right to request information about the personal information we collect and how it is shared. Since we do not collect personal information, there is no data to request or disclose under California law.

7. International Users

Solitairefun is operated from the United States. If you are accessing our Site from outside the United States, please be aware that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States where our servers are located.

By using our Site, you consent to the transfer of information to countries outside of your country of residence, which may have different data protection rules than your country.

We comply with applicable data protection laws and take appropriate safeguards to protect your privacy in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page
  • Providing notice on our homepage for significant changes

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your privacy. Your continued use of the Site after we post changes constitutes your acceptance of those changes.
